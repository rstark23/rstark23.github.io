Welcome

Doughnut Delights has been serving C-U with the highest quality doughnuts and coffees since 1971. Nestled in downtown Urbana, Doughnut Delights has become a haven for “Gotta have it” breakfast treats. Our doughnuts are baked fresh twice daily and our coffee’s brewed every fifteen minutes. If you have never been to C-U’s hidden gem then you are missing out. All month long Melvin's giving out free samples of this customer favorite from 9-11am. So stop on by and take a delicious Pinwheel for a spin.
